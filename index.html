Tackling Food Wastage in India

India wastes approximately 67 million tonnes of food annually, while millions go hungry. Our platform connects food producers with surplus food to consumers who need it, reducing wastage and fighting hunger simultaneously.

FoodConnects creates a seamless bridge between restaurants, caterers, and individuals with excess food and NGOs, shelters, and communities in need, ensuring fresh food reaches those who need it most within safe time limits.
